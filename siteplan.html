Site Name

Site Name: Rest Countries

Reason: This name reflects the primary purpose of the site, which is to provide comprehensive information about countries around the world.

Optional Domain Availability: country-Info-Hub.Netlify.app

Site Purpose

The site serves as a centralized resource for users to explore information about all countries, including flags, populations, regions, and more. It aims to educate users and provide a simple way to find specific country details.

Scenarios

Scenarios:

  • What information can I find about a specific country, like its population and capital?
  • How can I view all the countries and their flags quickly?
